Questions

Good things to know.

How do I book a session?

Just send us an email at russellp0105@gmail.com. Tell us what you have in mind and we'll take it from there.

When will I get my photos?

We deliver your edited gallery within 7 to 14 days of the session. You'll get a link to download everything in full resolution.

Where do you shoot?

We're based in Oceanside and shoot all over San Diego County. The beach, a park, your backyard — if you have a spot in mind, we're open to it.

Can I request specific shots?

Absolutely. Send us any inspiration or must-have shots ahead of time and we'll do our best to make them happen.

What if the weather is bad?

We'll reschedule at no charge. We want your photos to look great as much as you do.

Do you edit all the photos?

Yes. Every photo in your gallery is individually edited before delivery. No raw, unedited images in your final set.
